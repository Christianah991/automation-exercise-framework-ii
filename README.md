## Six User Journeys

1. User Registration
2. User Login and Logout
3. Product Search
4. Add to Cart and Checkout
5. Place Order and Payment
6. Cart Management

## Four Chosen Journeys

**1. User Registration**

This journey is the gateway to the platform. Without it, no new customer can 
create an account, access their order history or complete a purchase. If 
registration breaks, the business loses potential customers at the very first 
interaction, making it one of the most important journeys to keep working at 
all times.

**2. Place Order and Payment**

This is the most business critical journey on the site. Any defect here 
directly results in lost revenue because a customer who cannot complete a 
purchase is a lost sale. It also spans the widest range of functionality end 
to end, from product selection and checkout through to payment and invoice 
generation, giving strong coverage in a single journey.

**3. Product Search and Cart Verification After Login**

This journey validates that items added to the cart before login are still 
present once the user authenticates. It also confirms that search returns the 
correct results, which is typically the primary way users discover products 
on the site.

**4. Cart Management**

The cart is central to the purchasing experience and users interact with it 
constantly throughout their session. It is important to verify that adding 
and removing items works as expected, as any issues here can prevent users 
from completing their order and undermine trust in the platform as a whole.
